Unit 9: Design Critique

Step 1: Reflection

Exisiting websites that most inspired my designs are mostly blogs. A few that are at top of my mind are Notcot and Summer Tomato.

I'm just really proud that I was able to create a website! I like the colors I chose and how simple and clean it looks. I also like that it is responsive!

I really enjoyed creating wireframes. I had so many ideas, I found myself struggling with sticking to one design.

It was fun turning my design into a webpage. Unfortunately, I didn't have the time to create what I had laid out originally in my wireframe. I had fun with the challenge though. :)

I learned that there were a lot of possibilities for design for different formats. I had never really given a website's responsiveness a thought. I liked that we got the chance to make our projects responsive.


Step 2: Reviews

Squarespace.com

I love the clean look to this website and the flow to the site. The layout is simple and clean. The images are carefully chosen to help with the site's branding. I would love to build something like this one day. Navigation is easy and overall flow is great!

cavs.nit.edu

The moment the page appeared on my screen my eyes were drawn to several moving objects on the screen. As a person trying to view the site, I was quickly annoyed at the gifs. The layout was all over the place and I found my eyes darting all over the page with anxiety. I would say that the overall flow of the website is poor. I found the site hard to navigate and the content hard to read, as the font size was too small.
